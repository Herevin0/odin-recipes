🥚 Fried and maybe burned eggs 🥚

Description

In this recipe you are gonna learn how to cook fried and maybe burned eggs, I hope you don't harm yourself in the process :)

Ingredients

  • eggs *-* 🥚🥚🥚🥚🥚🥚🥚
  • all your cooking skills
  • a nonstick pan (if you really want to make it right, I highly recommend it)
  • a fat: butter/olive oil/vegetable oil/bacon fat. Wathever you like

Steps

  1. Put your nonstick pan over medium heat and melt butter (or heat oil, you choose)
  2. Crack egg into pan with all your cooking skills
  3. Cook until white is completely set
  4. If you want to cook the other side, flip the egg with all your cooking skills and try to not harm yourself
  5. Wait the yolk gets completely set or as you please. JUST DON'T LET IT BURN, PAY ATTENTION TO YOUR MARVELOUS EGG, YOU ARE ALMOST DOING IT!!!
  6. Remove from pan and season the egg.
  7. Eat it nham nham deliciousssss
